About S&J Grab

In 2007, Jackie Marshall founded S&J Grab Limited, proving that a woman can make it in what is still today, a man's industry. Over the years, she has grown the business from a single grab wagon to a large fleet of vehicles and plant machinery. Through her perseverance against animosity and her dedication to providing a great service, she has proven that S&J Grab are a leading company in utility servicing, ground works, aggregate supplies and spoil removal.

Still growing and finding new ways to support the building and roadwork industries, this family firm is large enough to take on the biggest jobs, yet small enough to take pride and care in all their work.
